Kaomoji (顔文字; literally, "face mark") are Japanese emoticons you can understand without tilting your head sideways.

Double-clicking the kaomoji to the right will copy them to your clipboard. Certain characters may not display properly if your browser doesn't handle the full range of Unicode. (Hint: This site is a lot more fun in Firefox.)
